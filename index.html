What is Texas Hold'em?

Texas Hold'em is the most popular and widely played poker variant in the world, featured in both casual home games and high-stakes tournaments like the World Series of Poker. The game is a blend of strategy, psychology, and calculated risk-taking, making it appealing to both beginners and seasoned players. Each player is dealt two private cards (hole cards), while five community cards are placed face-up on the table in three stages: the flop (three cards), the turn (one card), and the river (one final card). The goal is to make the best possible five-card hand using any combination of hole and community cards. Betting rounds occur between each stage, allowing players to fold, call, raise, or bluff their way to victory.
