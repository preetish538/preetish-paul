What is Texas Hold'em?

Texas Hold'em is the most popular form of poker in the world. Players compete by making the best five-card hand using two private cards and five community cards. The game involves skill, strategy, and a bit of luck.

From casual games with friends to high-stakes tournaments like the World Series of Poker, Texas Hold'em has captivated millions worldwide.
